Heyo, My name is Benjamin. You can call me Ben, or Benny if you are feeling frisky. Professionally I’ve been doing system and solutions engineering. By Night, or with whatever spare time I can find, I climb down various tech rabbit holes.

A normal day of mine consists mostly of routines with a sprinkle of flair. I work out 2 hours every day, eat a mostly meat diet and multitask music, podcasts, movies and shows. My favorite hobbies are travelling, hiking and on the rare ocassion inebriated dancing. All the while I am doing research and working on hobby IT projects. Currently I'm working web design, machine learning, network security and various automation projects. As you can probably see, I'm all over the place.

Early Life

I consider myself insanely lucky to be born and raised by two amazing humans. My parents instilled important principles that still fuel my life to this day. Hard work, honesty and kindness. These three principles guide my actions each and every day. They also introduced the initial spark into the tech world, which was the original game boy and the family PC. Those were the first of many tech systems I wanted to master and share the knowledge i've gained to empower my friends and family.

College

I attended two different colleges, At Linn Tech I received my associates in computer programming and at SIU carbondale I studied information systems and network security. SIU Carbondale is where I had my first real job at the Daily Egyption. The DE was a great place to learn about deadlines, priorities and design. Many grueling hours and hardships but made tons of lasting memories. If it wasn’t for being a college part time gig, I would have loved to keep working there.

Career

After school I moved to Chicago and started my career in IT. I worked for two great companies where I learned a wide variety of systems in a support position. In 2016 I accepted a position with Stifel and ran several major projects starting with a windows 7 to 10 migration. I’m currently a System Engineer with Stifel working on all things Server and Workstation management. On a daily basis I'm performing vulnerability remediations, automating business workflows, packaging and deploying applications and imaging solutions. Current and Future projects are revolved around cloud computing and data analytics.

Motivation

I thrive in environments where there are opportunities to learn and grow. I’m always pushing for what is next, what challenges await me. I won’t always succeed but I learn from my failures and master from repetition. Knowledge also empowers me, I've always got an eye on the news of the day and the amazing research of tomorrow and beyond. I have great admiration for Elon Musk, Joe Rogan, and Lex Fridmun. I like to believe we share similar characteristcs in our passion for the work we do and the enthusiasim to help those n needi and share the knowledge we accumulate.
